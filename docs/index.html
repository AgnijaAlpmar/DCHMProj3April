Building plan drawings of Drottningholms palace theatre

Home | Collection |

 

This project is made for the course Digitising Cultural Heritage Material given by University of Borås and is an attempt of digitization of building plan drawings of Drottningholms palace theatre. It started as an interest to find initial architectural drawings, pictures and plans of Déjeuner Salon, a later annex to Drottningholms Palace Theatre in neo-classicism style, made by Louis Jean Desprez, opened 1791. The images of architectural plans cover the stages from the early planning of the current theatre, to be built on the ruins of the tragically burnt down theatre, to the later annex, based on the existing theatre building.

The pictures, was taken by Agnija Alpmar, student at master's programme: Library and Information Science, Digital Library and Information Services
